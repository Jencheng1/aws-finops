The FinOps Challenge

Complex Billing Structures
AWS offers 200+ services with complex pricing models, making it difficult to track and forecast costs accurately.
Unexpected Cost Spikes
Dynamic resource usage and auto-scaling can lead to unexpected cost increases that are difficult to detect in real-time.
Resource Waste
Idle or oversized resources account for up to 35% of cloud spend, but identifying them requires deep technical analysis.
Poor Cost Allocation
Inconsistent tagging and cost allocation practices make it difficult to attribute costs to specific teams or projects.
Tool Fragmentation
Multiple disconnected cost management tools create silos of information and require specialized expertise.
